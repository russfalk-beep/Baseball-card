Printing Tips

  • Use cardstock paper (110 lb / 200 gsm) for best results
  • Set your printer to "Actual Size" (not "Fit to Page")
  • For double-sided: select "Flip on Long Edge" in printer settings
  • Enable "Print Background Colors and Images" in your browser
  • Cut along the crop marks with a paper cutter for clean edges
